Bob Bob Bo Bo (Chinese: 柏伯伯, 1 September 1939 – 2 September 1945), commonly known and often referred to as Bob, was an elephant shaped gummy bear[1] made in China[2][3] that is green and non-existent[3]. A common misconception is that it is a delicious biscuit[3]. However, this is not true[3]. Born on the first day of World War Two, he made friends with the Nazis, who are thought to be non-existent[3] as well. On the first day of his life, Bob helped to start the Second World War. Afterwards, he allegedly ate Adolf Hitler, the Führer of Germany, on 2 September 1945, causing the end of World War Two. Unfortunately, he died of food poisoning[3] shortly after, but his legacy lives on till today[citation needed].
